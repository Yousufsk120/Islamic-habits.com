FAQ

What is included in this Quran vocabulary list?

The page includes 600 frequently used Quranic Arabic words with Arabic text, transliteration, English translation, and Bengali translation. The words are grouped into practical categories so learners can study by theme.

How should I memorize these 600 words?

Use a small daily target. Five words a day is enough to complete the list in about 120 days. Review them during recitation, Salah, and short revision sessions instead of trying to finish large sections at once.

Who is this page for?

This resource is useful for beginners, Bengali-speaking learners, students of Quran, and anyone trying to understand common Quranic vocabulary more directly without needing a full dictionary every time.
