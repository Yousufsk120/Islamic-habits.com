Digital Discipline Before Sleep

A night routine for protecting the soul from noise and waking with more strength.

Daily reminder 3 minute read Practice today

It is He who made the night for you as clothing and sleep as rest.

Qur'an 25:47

The last input matters

The soul carries the flavor of what it consumes. If the last hour is filled with noise, comparison, and speed, the morning becomes heavier. Let the final input be Qur'an, dua, gratitude, or silence.

Move the phone away

A phone beside the pillow is an invitation to negotiate with your own intention. Charge it away from the bed and use a separate alarm if possible.

Sleep as worship preparation

A believer can turn sleep into preparation for worship by intention. Rest is not laziness when it helps you rise for Fajr, work honestly, and serve people well.

Practice today

  • Put the phone outside arm's reach tonight.
  • Read one short dua before sleeping.
  • End the day by naming three blessings.
