The Meaning of Returning After Missing a Day

A hopeful reminder that spiritual growth is built through tawbah and return.

Daily reminder 3 minute read Practice today

Do not despair of the mercy of Allah.

Qur'an 39:53

Do not let one miss become a season

The danger is not only missing a habit. The danger is believing that missing means you no longer belong on the path. Return quickly, even with a small deed.

Tawbah is movement

Tawbah is not only feeling bad. It is turning back to Allah with honesty, stopping what harms you, and taking the next right step.

Small deeds reopen the road

Two rak'ahs, one page, one sincere dua, one apology, one charity: Allah can place enormous light in a small return.

Practice today

  • Make istighfar slowly ten times.
  • Restart one habit at its smallest version.
  • Do not delay the next prayer.
