A Small Dua Routine After Salah

A compact after-prayer routine that protects gratitude, forgiveness, and direction.

Daily reminder 3 minute read Practice today

Remember Me; I will remember you. Be grateful to Me and do not be ungrateful.

Qur'an 2:152

Do not rush away

After Salah, the heart is already standing near remembrance. Even two quiet minutes can change the texture of the day. Sit, breathe, and let the prayer leave a mark.

Ask for help worshipping well

A beautiful Prophetic dua asks Allah for help in remembering Him, thanking Him, and worshipping Him with excellence. This makes worship itself a gift we ask Allah to protect.

Make one personal request

After the fixed remembrances, add one honest dua in your own language. Ask Allah about what is actually heavy on your heart.

Practice today

  • Stay seated for two minutes after one prayer today.
  • Ask Allah for gratitude, forgiveness, and guidance.
  • Write down one dua you want to repeat for a week.
