Micro SaaS concept

Circle3: small circles for better Islamic habits.

A lightweight AI-assisted companionship system where Muslims are matched into intelligent three-person circles for habit building, accountability, reflection, emotional support, productivity, and faith-centered companionship.

Habit partner Reflection friend Circle guide

Faith-centered micro-community

You were never meant to grow alone.

Circle3 is not social media, not a lecture app, and not an open group chat. It is a quiet structure for three people to show up daily, build consistent Islamic habits, and encourage one another with adab.

Daily Circle Room 3 present
AI prompt active Moderated and purposeful
Prompt Which habit improved your iman recently, and what made it easier to repeat?
Member Fajr became easier when I slept earlier and kept my phone outside the bed.
Silent presence I am here silently today. Please make dua for my consistency.
01

Smart matching

Circles form around timezone, language, age band, activity level, personality, and goals like Fajr, Qur'an, study, fitness, or social media detox.

02

Daily guided rooms

Each room receives a halal conversation starter, a reflection task, and a small action so the group does not drift into empty chatting.

03

Habit dashboard

Track Salah, Qur'an minutes, dhikr, sleep, water, workout, detox, mood, and group encouragement without making faith feel like pressure.

04

Silent presence

Members can say “I am here silently” when they need companionship without talking. Presence still protects motivation.

Civilization bridge

The human search for the One God

Islam teaches that Allah sent messengers to peoples through history. This area should help users compare civilizations with humility: tawhid, prophecy, fitrah, worship, ethics, family, law, beauty, and the search for ultimate truth.
