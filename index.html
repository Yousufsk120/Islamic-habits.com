Micro SaaS concept

Circle3: small circles for better Islamic habits.

A lightweight AI-assisted companionship system where Muslims are matched into intelligent three-person circles for habit building, accountability, reflection, emotional support, productivity, and faith-centered companionship.

Habit partner Reflection friend Circle guide

Faith-centered micro-community

You were never meant to grow alone.

Circle3 is not social media, not a lecture app, and not an open group chat. It is a quiet structure for three people to show up daily, build consistent Islamic habits, and encourage one another with adab.

Private Circle Room Trust first

The problem

Many young Muslims are surrounded by content, but still growing alone.

Circle3 is designed for digital isolation, motivation crashes, inconsistent Salah and Qur'an routines, passive learning, and the missing feeling of trusted companionship. It gives the user a small circle that can be present without becoming noisy social media.

Digital isolation Habit inconsistency Low accountability Passive content

The solution

Three people, one guided room, one meaningful action each day.

The system combines habit tracking, structured interaction, AI-generated prompts, and adab-first moderation. It is not Facebook, Discord, Telegram, or a lecture app. It becomes faith-centered micro-community infrastructure.

Daily reflection Islamic habits Quiet support Guided growth

How Circle3 works

Profile, match, prompt, practice, repeat.

A user creates a simple profile, chooses Islamic and life goals, and receives a small circle built around compatibility. The circle then receives daily conversation starters, a habit check, and a low-pressure way to stay present.

01

User layer

Nickname, gender, country, language, timezone, goals, personality, and habit focus.

02

Matching engine

Groups three members by age band, goals, timezone, language, activity, and interests.

03

Daily room

Fajr Warriors, Qur'an Consistency, Student Productivity, or Muslim Entrepreneurs.

04

Growth loop

Prompt, reflection, habit streak, silent presence, encouragement, and next action.

Triadic activation engine

Real conversations begin only when three real people are present.

Circle3 unlocks chat, voice, habit sync, and the daily prompt only when three verified members enter the same room. Until then, users wait in a calm lobby. This creates authenticity, anticipation, anti-spam protection, and real social energy.

  • Three verified users required before a room becomes active.
  • Lobby mode keeps the experience quiet until the circle is complete.
  • Presence signals focus on safety, consent, and anti-abuse protection.
01

Mobile verification

One phone number, one account, with OTP through Twilio, MSG91, or Firebase Auth for the first launch.

02

Liveness check

A consent-based selfie or short liveness check helps reduce fake accounts, duplicate profiles, and bot circles.

03

Device trust

Device, IP, and browser risk signals can protect the platform from spam farms without exposing private details to other users.

04

Secure storage

Identity checks should use encrypted storage, minimal retention, secure face embeddings, and clear user consent.

Anti-harassment filters, gender separation options, AI moderation, spam protection, behavior scoring, and anonymous reporting.
